Privacy Policy for Coal Veer

Thank you for choosing Coal Veer, an innovative service provided by the Ministry of Coal. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Coal Veer service. By using Coal Veer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we handle it.

Information Collection and Use

Coal Veer may collect certain personally identifiable information that you provide voluntarily, such as your name, email address, contact number, and other details necessary for effective service delivery. We may also collect non-personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your device information, IP address, and usage patterns, for analytical and service improvement purposes.

Use of Information

The information collected by Coal Veer may be used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Respond to your inquiries, requests, and concerns.
  • Customize and enhance your experience with Coal Veer.
  • Communicate important service-related announcements and updates.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to external parties without your consent, except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share your information with authorized personnel within the Ministry of Coal or trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering Coal Veer services. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard the confidentiality and security of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Links to Government Resources

Coal Veer may contain links to government websites or services operated by the Ministry of Coal or other government entities. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these resources for further information on how your data is handled.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically, and any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any updates.
